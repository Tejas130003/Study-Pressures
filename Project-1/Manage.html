How students can manage their study pressure?

  1. Stops comparing your marks with your friends.
  2. Focus on your hobbies and favourite subjects.
  3. Have a walk in morning daily to feel better.
  4. Convience your parents about pursuing your passion as carrer.
  5. Share your problem to whom you believe.
  6. Never think yourself as small,useless,inferrior etc.
  7. Work hard in your studies,hobbies or any other
  8. Stop overthinking.
